Mac & Cheese

The mac and cheese recipe to rule them all. Make it for yourself!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz elbows
  • 16 oz shredded cheddar
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/8 tsp onion powder
  • 1 sleeve Ritz crackers

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Cook the elbows.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine elbows, shredded cheddar, cream of mushroom soup, milk, mayo, and onion powder. Mix well.
  4. Pour the mixture into a greased 9"x13" casserole dish.
  5. Top with crushed Ritz crackers.
  6. Bake for 40 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
